What is it for?

Keeping track of your health is very important, and with the help of our gadgets it will become much easier. Fitness bracelets can track training load, recovery time and training effect. These three factors help contextualize the effects of exercise on the body and provide a more complete picture, such as calories burned. VO2 max monitoring allows you to track your maximum oxygen consumption as well as continuous SpO2 monitoring to keep track of blood oxygen saturation levels. The fitness bracelet will also notify users if this indicator drops below 90%. Also included are heart rate monitoring, daily steps, distance, calorie burn, sleep tracking, stress tracking, and women's health.
